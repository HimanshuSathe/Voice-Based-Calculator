How to use it:

  • Addition--> To perform addition ('+') use only "Plus" ex. 4 plus 5
  • Subtraction--> To perform subtraction ('-') use only "Minus" ex. 4 minus 5
  • Multiplication--> To perform multiplication ('*') use only "Multiply" ex. 4 multiply 5
  • Division-->To perform divison ('/') use "divide / divided / divide by" ex. 4 divide 5
  • Remainder/Modular--> To perform remainder ('%') use "remainder" ex. 4 remainder 5
